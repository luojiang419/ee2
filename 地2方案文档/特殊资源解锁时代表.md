# Epoch–Resource Availability

In *Empire Earth II* (地球帝国2), special resources are advanced resources unlocked in specific epochs. They appear as unharvestable "rock piles" before their epoch and become harvestable when the epoch is reached. **Costs for a given epoch must only use resources that are available in that epoch.**

Official EE2 harvestable epoch ranges (per game design):

| Resource       | 中文   | Epoch range | Notes (EE2) |
|----------------|--------|-------------|-------------|
| FOOD           | 食物   | E1 onward   | (base)      |
| WOOD           | 木材   | E1 onward   | (base)      |
| STONE          | 石头   | E1 onward   | (base)      |
| GOLD           | 黄金   | E1 onward   | (base)      |
| **TIN**        | **锡** | **E1–E6 only** | Max 6 citizens per tin mine. Banned E7+. |
| **IRON**       | **铁** | **E4–E9 only** | Max 6 citizens per iron mine. Banned E10+. |
| **SALTPETER**  | **硝石** | **E7–E12 only** | Max 6 per deposit. Banned E13+. |
| **OIL**        | **石油** | **E10–E15 only** | Requires derrick on deposit; max 6. Banned E16+. |
| **URANIUM**    | **铀** | **E13–E15 only** | Requires refinery on deposit; max 6. Banned E16+. |

## Rules for costs

- **Epoch 1–3:** FOOD, WOOD, STONE, GOLD, TIN (E1–E6). No IRON, SALTPETER, OIL, URANIUM.
- **Epoch 4–6:** May also use IRON (E4–E9). TIN and IRON both allowed. No SALTPETER, OIL, URANIUM.
- **Epoch 7–9:** May use IRON, SALTPETER. No TIN (banned E7+). No OIL, URANIUM.
- **Epoch 10–12:** May use SALTPETER, OIL. No TIN, IRON (banned E10+). No URANIUM.
- **Epoch 13–15:** May use OIL, URANIUM. No TIN, IRON, SALTPETER (banned E13+).

When editing unit or tech costs in `dbtechtreenode.csv` or `upgrade_unittypes.csv`, set special-resource columns to 0 for epochs where that resource is not available.
